Mastodon for respectful technologists, geeks and nerds of every type and stripe.

Why choose vmst.io as your Mastodon instance?

  • We are party to the joinmastodon.org Server Covenant, and active contributors to the Mastodon project and wider community.
  • We respect your fundamental right to privacy. We have an A+ rating from Mozilla Observatory, and have no ads, third party cookies or trackers.
  • We're highly available, and take ownership of issues when we're not. We publish our realtime system status, and have a better than 99.9% observed availability from Instances.social.
  • We are well moderated, with multiple volunteer staffers around the world monitoring for user reports, with clear rules against hate speech and harassment.
  • Your Mastodon account here also gets you a blog on write.vmst.io and an vmst.io account on Matrix, all integrated through a single sign-on.
  • We host our own instances of alternative feature-rich web clients like Elk or accessibility focused ones like Semaphore.

When you apply for an account on vmst路io, we ask you to provide a reason why you want to join. We take this seriously. Tell us a bit about yourself, what brings you here, and what your interests are. It doesn't have to be formal, just enough to make us excited to welcome you to our community.
