Why choose vmst.io as your Mastodon instance?

  • We respect your fundamental right to privacy. We have an A+ rating from Mozilla Observatory, have no ads, third party cookies or trackers and use the best possible TLS encryption for all connections as verified by Cryptcheck and SSL Labs.
  • We're highly available, and take ownership of issues when we're not. We publish our realtime system status, and have a better than 99% observed availibility from Instances.social.
  • We are well moderated, with multiple volunteer staffers around the world monitoring for user reports, and clear rules against hate speech and harrassment.

When you apply for an account on vmst路io, we ask you to provide a reason why you want to join. We take this seriously. Tell us a bit about yourself, what brings you here, and what your interests are. It doesn't have to be formal, just enough to make us excited to welcome you to our community.
