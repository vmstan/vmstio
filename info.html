Mastodon for respectful technologists. Geeks and nerds of every type and stripe.

Why choose vmst.io as your Mastodon instance?

  • We are party to the joinmastodon.org Server Covenant, and active contributors to the Mastodon project and wider community.
  • We respect your fundamental right to privacy. We have an A+ rating from Mozilla Observatory, and have no ads, third party cookies or trackers.
  • We're highly available, and take ownership of issues when we're not. We publish our realtime system status, and have a better than 99.9% observed availability.
  • We are well moderated, with multiple volunteer staffers around the world monitoring for user reports, with clear rules against hate speech and harassment.
  • Your Mastodon account here also gets you an vmst.io account on Matrix, all integrated through a single sign-on.
  • We host our own instances of alternative feature-rich web clients like Elk or accessibility focused ones like Semaphore, and have built in support for Elephant!

When you apply for an account on vmst·io, we require you to verify you're a legitimate human being by verifying your email and completing an hCaptcha. Our staff may also manually flag your account for additional verification if required. For more information please refer to our New Accounts documentation.
