Mastodon for respectful technologists. Geeks and nerds of every type and stripe.

Why choose vmst.io as your Mastodon instance?

  • We run the latest available Mastodon code, usually before anyone else, and contribute back to the Mastodon project.
  • We're built to be highly performant and highly available, and take ownership of issues when we're not. We also publish our realtime system status, and strive for better than 99.9% observed availability.
  • We are well moderated, with multiple volunteer staffers around the world monitoring for user reports, with clear rules against hate speech and harassment. We proactively defederate from known bad actors to protect our users.
  • We have an A+ rating from Mozilla Observatory and Qualys SSL Labs, but most importantly we don't leverage any third party cookies, use trackers or run ads on our service, as verified by The Markup's Blacklight.

When you apply for an account on vmst·io, we require you to verify you're a legitimate human being by verifying your email and completing an hCaptcha. Our staff may also manually flag your account for additional verification if required. For more information please refer to our New Accounts documentation.
